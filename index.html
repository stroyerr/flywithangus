Instruction

01

Primary flight training

Structured, practical instruction for student pilots working toward confident and consistent aircraft control.

02

Flight reviews

A useful, scenario-based review designed to sharpen skills rather than simply check a box.

03

Rusty pilot refreshers

Supportive recurrent training for pilots who want to rebuild comfort, proficiency, and sound decision-making.

04

Cross-country coaching

Planning, weather, airspace, workload management, and real-world aeronautical decision-making.

Instruction in your aircraft, subject to insurance, operating limitations, and an appropriate instructor checkout.

About Angus

Professional standards without the flight-school feel.

I trained as a commercial pilot in Sydney before completing FAA commercial, instrument, and instructor training in Oregon. That experience taught me to adapt quickly, communicate clearly, and approach aviation from more than one regulatory perspective.

My goal is simple: make every flight useful. You should leave knowing what improved, what still needs work, and exactly how to move forward.
